What is web3?

Web3, like its predecessors, promises to help solve social problems linked to inequality with technology. One of the pillars of Web3 is financial decentralization. For this reason, crypto finance is its backbone. This proposal seems more associated with minority interests than with that of the majority. Some economists also speak of digital post-neoliberalism.
